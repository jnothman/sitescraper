Summary

SMS Management & Technology Limited (SMS) is an Australia-based company. The Company operates under two brands. Under the SMS brand, the Company offers a range of management and technology related business services, which include, Consulting and Project Management, Information Technology Services, Systems Integration, Business Transformation, Enterprise Solutions, Defense and Electronic Systems Solutions, and Operational Learning and Change. Under the M&T Resources brand it offers Recruitment and Contract Labour. On October 4, 2007, SMS acquired 100% of Total Learn Pty Ltd. This business specializes in operational learning and change, offering training and process documentation to aid in knowledge transfer. On March 26, 2007, SMS Consulting Group Ltd acquired Forward Consulting Pty Ltd (trading as Forward Media), and on April 16, 2007, it acquired Bondfern Pty Ltd. On April 23, 2007, SMS Consulting Group Ltd acquired Avoga Pty Ltd.
