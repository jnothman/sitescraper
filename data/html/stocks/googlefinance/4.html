Summary

Westpac Banking Corporation (Westpac) provides a range of banking and financial services, including retail, commercial and institutional banking and wealth management services. The Companys operations comprise five areas of business through which it serves approximately 7.1 million customers. It includes Business Financial Services, Consumer Financial Services, BT Financial Group Australia (BTFG), Westpac Institutional Bank and New Zealand Banking. On January 4, 2008, Westpac completed the acquisition of the RAMS franchise distribution business, which includes 99 stores, owned and operated by 54 franchisees.
