Summary

Woolworths Limited is a retail group that operates in Australia and New Zealand. The Company has approximately 3,000 stores. It operates 965 supermarkets under the Woolworths and Safeway brands in Australia, and under Woolworths, Foodtown and Countdown brands in New Zealand. Woolworths Limited’s liquor retailing services different customer needs through BWS, Dan Murphy and its Woolworths/Safeway attached liquor outlets. The Company operates in five business segments: Supermarket Group, which encompasses supermarkets, retail liquor outlets and petrol outlets; General Merchandise Group, which encompasses BIG W discount department stores; Consumer Electronics Group, which encompasses Dick Smith Electronics, Tandy and Dick Smith Electronics Powerhouse stores; Hotels Group, which encompasses on-premise liquor sales, food, accommodation, gaming and venue hire, and Wholesale Group, which comprises Statewide Independent Wholesalers (SIW).
