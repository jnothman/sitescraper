Summary

Kagara Ltd, formerly Kagara Zinc Limited, is principally engaged in mineral exploration, development and mineral production. During the fiscal year ended June 30, 2008 (fiscal 2008), the Company achieved copper production of 26,326 tons of copper from the Mt Garnet and Thalanga treatment facilities. Production from the Mt Garnet polymetallic plant, during fiscal 2008, was 40,940 tons of contained zinc, 3,636 tons of contained copper and 9,359 tons of contained lead. Mt Garnet copper circuit production, during fiscal 2008, was 7,264 tons of contained copper and the Thalanga plant produced 15,426 tons of contained copper. The Companys wholly owned subsidiaries include Mungana Pty Ltd, Kagara Copper Pty Ltd and Kagara Nickel Pty Ltd.
